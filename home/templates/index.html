Walkabout

The fast way to plan and review your delivery, canvassing and surveying campaigns.

Everything your team needs to deliver marketing literature, canvassing and surveys. Our technology will help you uncover insights to make your campaigns more effective.

See how our app can empower your campaigns:

  • Go paperless. No more paper-based walking lists, susceptible to weather or loss.

  • Provide agents with in-app maps and directions to campaign area.

  • Instantly see when an agent ticks off a street as complete.

  • Rounds automatically ticked off, once all related streets are completed.

  • Campaigns automatically ticked off, once all related rounds are completed.

  • Create selective address lists for targeted canvassing, surveying and delivery follow-ups.

  • No more time-consuming collation of campaign results recorded on paper.

  • Generate reports for missed addresses, not founds and unfinished rounds for follow-ups.

  • Generate statistical reports at organisation, campaign, round and street level for insights into campaign effectiveness and coverage.
